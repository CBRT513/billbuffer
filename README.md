# BillBuffer - Financial Peace Through Simplicity

## What is BillBuffer?

BillBuffer answers one question: **"How much of my paycheck is for bills, and how much is for me?"**

No budgeting. No tracking. No bank connections. Just clarity.

## Core Philosophy

**"We don't know you exist."** Once you download BillBuffer, we have no idea who you are, what you earn, or what you owe. Your financial data stays on your device, encrypted, private, and completely under your control.

## Who Is This For?

- **The Smart but Lazy:** High intelligence, low financial patience
- **The Anxious Avoider:** Money causes stress, needs permission to spend
- **The Forgotten Bill Victim:** Forgets quarterly/annual bills, pays late fees
- **The Trust-Nothing User:** Won't use bank-connected apps, values privacy above features

## What Makes BillBuffer Different?

1. **Completely Offline** - Works in airplane mode, no internet required
2. **No Financial Jargon** - We say "money for bills" not "allocated to fixed expenses"
3. **Handles ALL Bills** - Monthly, quarterly, annual - we remember what you forget
4. **Privacy First** - No accounts, no tracking, no data collection
5. **Psychological Safety** - Reduces anxiety through clarity, not lectures
