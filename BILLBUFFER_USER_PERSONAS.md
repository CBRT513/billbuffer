# BillBuffer User Personas

## Persona 1: "The Smart Lazy"
*This is the founder's persona*

**Characteristics:**
- High intelligence, low financial patience
- Wants optimization without effort
- Hates being late but hates tracking more
- Would pay money to not think about money
- Tech-savvy but finance-averse

**Their Truth:** "I could build a complex spreadsheet. I just won't maintain it."

**What They Need:**
- Set-it-and-forget-it solution
- Minimal ongoing maintenance
- Clear, simple answer to "what's safe to spend?"

**Current Solution:**
- Mental math and hoping
- Occasional spreadsheet attempts (abandoned)
- Accepting occasional late fees as the cost of simplicity

## Persona 2: "The Anxious Avoider"

**Characteristics:**
- Capable but overwhelmed
- Money causes physical stress response
- Avoids looking at accounts
- Purchases create guilt spirals
- Needs permission to spend

**Their Truth:** "I make good money but feel broke because I don't know what's mine."

**What They Need:**
- Clear permission to spend
- Reduced decision fatigue
- No scary financial terms
- Gentle, non-judgmental interface

**Current Solution:**
- Avoidance and anxiety
- Checking account balance obsessively
- Never feeling safe to spend

## Persona 3: "The Forgotten Bill Victim"

**Characteristics:**
- Generally organized but imperfect
- Quarterly bills are their nemesis
- Good intentions, human memory
- Late fees from forgetting, not poverty
- Wants to be responsible

**Their Truth:** "I'm not bad with money, I just forget irregular bills exist."

**What They Need:**
- System that remembers everything
- Accommodation for non-monthly bills
- No shame for forgetting
- Simple way to capture ALL bills

**Current Solution:**
- Calendar reminders (often ignored)
- Sticky notes
- Surprise bills and scrambling

## Persona 4: "The Trust-Nothing User"
*KEY MARKET - Underserved*

**Characteristics:**
- Been burned by data breaches
- Reads every permission request
- Uses cash when possible
- Suspicious of "free" apps
- Knows nothing is really private
- Still has bills to manage

**Their Fears:**
- "Apps sell my data"
- "Hackers will get my bank info"
- "They're watching my spending"
- "AI is learning my patterns"
- "Nothing is really deleted"

**Their Truth:** "I'd rather pay late fees than give apps my financial data."

**What They Need:**
- Provable privacy (not just promises)
- Complete offline functionality
- No account creation required
- Visible data location (device only)
- Clear data deletion option

**Current Solution:**
- Paper and pen
- Maybe a local spreadsheet
- Cash envelope system
- Mental tracking
- Accepting late fees as privacy cost

## The Anti-Persona: Who We DON'T Build For

**The Financial Optimizer:**
- Wants detailed budgets
- Tracks every penny
- Loves categories and reports
- Seeks investment advice
- Already uses YNAB happily

**Why We Don't Build for Them:** They're already served by complex tools and don't need our simplicity.
